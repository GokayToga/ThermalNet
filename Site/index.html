Welcome to ThermalNet

TEDU CMPE491/492 Senior Project

ThermalNet is a deep learning-based system designed to enhance feature matching between RGB and thermal images, addressing challenges in low-light and low-texture environments. The project aims to optimize real-time feature alignment by leveraging multi-scale feature extraction, coarse-to-fine matching, and sub-pixel refinement techniques. Developed in collaboration with Raventech and Gürbağ Defense, ThermalNet's applications span defense, surveillance, aerial monitoring, and navigation, offering improved accuracy and usability in thermal image analysis.
